Why request an Internship?

Atlanta International School provides a Science, Technology, Engineering, and Math (STEM) or STEAM (with Arts) diploma endorsement to recognize students who have followed a STE(A)M- rich curriculum as well as STE(A)M-related co-curricular experiences within the context of the International Baccalaureate Program. Students who receive an AIS STEM or STEAM endorsement at graduation have demonstrated an interest in and a commitment to solving global issues of the 21st century through the mission of the school in relation to Science, Technology, Engineering, Math and Arts.
